# Shipping Policy

We are proud to offer **Worldwide Shipping** on all orders.

## Shipping Rates
* **Worldwide Shipping**: Free / Included on all items!

## Delivery Times
* **US & Europe**: 7-12 business days
* **Rest of the World**: 10-15 business days

All orders are processed and shipped within 1-3 business days. Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email to monitor your delivery status in real-time.
