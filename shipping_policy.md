# Shipping Policy

We ship worldwide. Because our items are sourced from partner suppliers, please review the timelines below before ordering.

## Shipping Rates

* **Standard Shipping**: Free on all orders.

## Processing Time

Orders are processed within **1–3 business days**. During busy periods or sales this may take slightly longer.

## Delivery Times

Delivery times are estimates and begin after your order has been processed and dispatched:

* **United States & Europe**: 7–15 business days
* **Rest of the World**: 10–20 business days

Please note that these are estimates, not guarantees. Customs processing, weather, carrier delays, and remote destinations can extend delivery times beyond the ranges above.

## Tracking

Once your order ships, we'll email you a tracking number. Tracking may take a few days to show its first scan after dispatch.

## Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to import duties or taxes charged by your country's customs authority. These charges are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in our prices.

## Questions

If you have any questions about your shipment, email us at **rodofheart11@gmail.com**.
